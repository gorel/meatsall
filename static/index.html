Rating philosophy

  • 0 (meats none) - didn't even give us food
  • 1 (meats some) - gave us food but it was bad and I couldn't finish it
  • 2 (meats most) - food was good, but not better than the average meal I cook at home
  • 3 (meats all) - food was good, better than the average meal I cook at home, and I'd be happy having a meal like this every day
  • 4 (exceeds) - food was great, better than most meals I cook at home, and I'm thinking about getting seconds
  • 5 (greatly exceeds) - food was phenomenal, better than anything I've ever cooked at home, and I'm getting seconds even if I'm full
  • 6 (redefines) - This is literally the best thing I've ever eaten and now the rating philosophy needs to be updated
